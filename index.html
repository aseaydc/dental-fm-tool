Supplement Recommendations
Split into two categories: supplements within dental scope of practice (dentist prescribes directly for oral conditions) and systemic needs identified by oral findings (refer to physician or functional medicine practitioner).
Scope of Practice: In most U.S. states including North Carolina, dentists may prescribe supplements and vitamins directly related to the treatment of a dental or oral condition. Systemic interventions require referral to a licensed medical provider.
🤰 PREGNANCY FLAG ACTIVE: Supplement recommendations have been adjusted for safety. Verify all supplements with the patient's OB/GYN.
